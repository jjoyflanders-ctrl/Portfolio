The Brand

Highlight Industries is a manufacturer of stretch wrap equipment and quality control systems. Growth in technology and specialization has helped the company stay ahead of competitors. Highlight offers one of the largest selections of stretch wrap equipment in the world, with distributors worldwide and a strong focus on quality, value, and technology.

The Goal

Improve internal processes while elevating both digital and print communication. The focus was on making information easier to access, improving consistency across materials, and strengthening Highlight’s digital presence.

The Vibes

Clean, practical, and technical, while still modern and approachable. The work focused on clarity, usability, and consistency across digital and print materials.

Outcome

  • Made contact sharing easier with personalized digital business cards
  • Improved consistency through updated and automated email signatures
  • Introduced Quick Start Guides and QR codes to make manuals easier for customers to access
  • Saved internal time and reduced reliance on printed documentation
  • Improved Shopify organization and product clarity
  • Contributed to Shopify improvements that supported 157% total sales growth Increased gross sales 127% year-over-year
  • Helped reduce shipment-related issues through better product data accuracy
