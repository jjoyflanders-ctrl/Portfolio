The Brand

Kenowa Hills Public Schools is a northwest Grand Rapids district serving approximately 2,850 students from preschool through 12th grade, focused on personalized learning and strong community connections.

The Goal

Strengthen PBIS across the middle school and support district alignment through clearer communication, student engagement, and data-informed decision making.

The Vibes

Student-centered, supportive, and approachable. Communication focused on clarity, consistency, and meeting middle school students where they are.

Outcome

  • Served as PBIS Tier 1 Co-Chair
  • Developed GOLD acronym adopted district-wide
  • Reduced discipline incidents
  • Increased PBIS fidelity
  • Staff participation increased from 30% to 90%
  • Improved communication and consistency
